PortKey is the easiest way to share HTML & CSS.
No server, terminal, or file transfer client required.

"Installation"

1. Drag PortKey into your bookmarks bar.
2. There is no second step.

How to use it.

1. Open the page you want to share in your browser.
2. Click on the portkey bookmark.

Portkey will automagically upload the page in the background and give you a short link to share.

Important Answers

Can I host a site or application on Portkey?
No. Portkey is for temporary, static HTML & CSS.

Will Portkey work with my local HTML & CSS files?
Yes. That includes anything served by localhost.
If your browser can render it, Portkey can serve it.

What about style changes made in the inspector?
Portkey will sync those changes as well.

What about javascript and images?
Portkey won't host images, and removes javascript.
Externally hosted images will work just fine.
